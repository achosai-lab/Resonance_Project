Title: Breathing Structure / 構造の呼吸
Style: city pop × ambient soul × analog minimalism × tape warmth
Tempo: mid to slow range, natural flow without strict bpm
Vocal: soft low female vocal, breathy tone, detached but human
Mood: nostalgic, hazy, imperfect calm, faint hope
Instrumentation:
- Rhodes piano (warm uneven touch, tape wobble)
- sub-bass (soft, deep, sometimes delayed)
- analog drum (loose rimshot, relaxed hats)
- light chorus guitar or electric piano layer
- faint theremin-like synth (appears irregularly)
- granular or modular pad (unstable pitch)
- ambient noise layer, subtle breathing texture
Mix: analog stereo, minimal reverb, slight tape hiss
Dynamics: gentle compression, natural rise and fade
Atmosphere: between stillness and motion, twilight emotion
Notes:
- allow timing offset and natural drift
- balance rhythm and silence
- avoid quantized perfection
- let tone and air create meaning
