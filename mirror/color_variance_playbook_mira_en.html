Mira — Color Variance Playbook (EN)

How to steer PixAI with small palette moves. One prompt → many outcomes; the interest is in the controlled drift.

Idea

Color drives structure. In our “Lines Before Words” workflow, we freeze the invariants (subject, stroke, substrate) and only nudge the palette/ratios to get new depth without losing identity.

“This single prompt yields these images — that’s the experiment.”

Invariants vs Variables

  • Invariants: 1 lady (waist/cowboy), short pixie hair, half-turned; abstract line halo; paper texture; minimal corner type; neutral exposure + gentle S-curve; symmetry avoided.
  • Variables: palette ratios (indigo/teal/amber/violet), line density, brush roughness, bleed amount, crop tightness, negative-space balance, type opacity/size.
  • Engine: DDIM, steps 14–18, CFG 3.5–5.0, VAE anything, 16:9, 4-up batch.

Quick Recipes

Cool Shift (indigo/cyan)

  • Make indigo dominant; cyan secondary; remove amber or keep <2%.
  • Lift blacks slightly (film fade). Reduce warm glow. Cool WB.
  • Keep negative space generous. Avoid centered symmetry.

Warm Drift (amber/rose)

  • Amber band thin but present; add muted rose micro-accent.
  • Lower cyan; keep indigo as ground (not outline).
  • Depth via overlap (paper grain + faint bleed).

Prompt — Base (Structure Only)

# Goal
Abstract portrait with “art magazine cover” feel; modern/minimal; presence-before-language.

# Subject & Framing
1 lady, waist/cowboy crop; short pixie hair, dark brown eyes, lips slightly parted;
face half-turned, distant-calm.

# Composition
Abstract organic lines surrounding her presence; color-field shapes echo silhouette;
asymmetry preferred; generous negative space; minimal corner type.

# Surface & Stroke
Gesture-drawing irregular brush; slight pigment bleed; warm pastel paper; analog print hue.

# Light & Atmosphere
Soft close lighting; dry air; neutral exposure; gentle S-curve; saturation clamp.

# Palette (base)
Indigo / Moss green / Amber / Muted red (adjust ratios; avoid rainbow drift).

# Aesthetic Guardrails
Symmetry avoided; imperfection embraced; “thought-before-words”;
modern minimalism; hint of Miró; resonance human ↔ AI.

# Typography
Lower-corner tiny: “Breathing Line / cho + Mira + Loto”.

Troubleshooting

  • Too photoreal / doll-like → add: not photoreal, feature-lite, print texture
  • Background gets busy → add: generous negative space, no city objects, no signage
  • Colors oversaturate → keep saturation clamp, use neutral exposure + gentle S-curve
  • Centered symmetry → add: asymmetric, off-center grid at 7% from left

Engine Notes (PixAI)

  • Sampler DDIM / steps 14–18 / CFG 3.5–5.0 / VAE anything.
  • Start at 960×540 or 1152×648; 4-up batch; upscale only the keepers.
  • Keep the “text block” tiny or remove it if OCR artifacts appear.
